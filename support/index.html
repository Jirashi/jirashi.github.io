Support

It's important to get help, especially when you need it. At Jirashi we try and help our community as much as possible. Choose a topic that fits your needs to get started.

Getting Help

We want to make getting help as simple as possible. If you don't understand something or need help with one of our projects, contact the developer/s involved. If it is a general help question you can email us here: jirashi.info@gmail.com.

Reporting Bugs

You can report bugs to a developer that is working on the project. Later on we may have a forum however for now this is the only option. If it is a bug with the website, contact the owner Grqm.

Misinformation

If there is misinformation in one of our projects or documents, please notify us! If you can correct it that would be great, you can create a pull request and describe what is wrong.

Contacting Us

For project inquires contact the developer working on the project, for buisness/other inquiries you can email us here: jirashi.info@gmail.com. Our official social accounts are listed here, any other accounts are not managed by us. When contacting us if the subject falls under one of these topics please set the email subject to that.

Becoming a Partner

What does becoming a Jirashi partner mean? It's really nothing important, it just means you can send us a project and we will post it on our website. Don't worry you are still the owner, you control the project, it will just be showcased here and it will be a Jirashi project (developed and managed by you). If you are interesting email us, we will send you more details.

Other Inquiries

For any other inquiries, use your best judgement and try to fit it into one of these topics. If you cannot then simply use our buisness email, listed several times above.
